Crown
A crown or cap is a solution to improve the shape of your tooth.

It is used to protect the underlying tooth from further damage.

Example of the cases indicated for crowns are cracked tooth, badly broken tooth due to decay or trauma, restoring tooth after root canal treatment, as part of a bridge and cosmetic modification.

We have full metal, full porcelain and porcelain fused to metal crowns.

The procedure to place a crown on your tooth is straight forward and painless!
