Scaling&Polishing
Scaling removes tartar and food stuck in teeth and gums. Polishing removes stubborn stains caused by smoking, tea & coffee and other habits.

Our scaling & polishing provides you fresh breath and pristine clean teeth in a comfortable way.

Treatment is customized to your needs, ie. warm water irrigation & numbing for patients with sensitive teeth, fluorite paste polishing for high caries risk and air polishing for heavy smokers.
