Teeth Extraction
Saving a tooth is always better in dental perspective. Our main job as dentist is to preserve the health of the tooth and the tissues surrounding it.

In some case however, the tooth cannot be saved anymore, so extraction is the only solution.

Extraction is also indicated in cases like symptomatic non-functional tooth.

The tooth may sometimes need to be removed surgically. For instance, removal of impacted wisdom tooth.

This process will be done as quick as possible.
